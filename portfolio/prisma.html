PRISMA Framework

An open framework to guide projects from start to finish.

Brief

The three reasons that encouraged the framework creation.

Need

Created by me and three friends (Gabriel, Rafael and Bruno) due to the need to deliver projects with performance and fidelity.

Co-creation

User centered framework based on waves by different stages (think, build, test, validate and follow). It was built through co-creation.

Opportunity

Opportunity to validate the framework in a pilot project.

Sketching

Sketch of the process of the model that maps each stage of the project. Framework flow improvement, creating a modular but free design process.

The framework

Insire the framework process.

Think

The process starts with the act of thinking as discovery, research and understanding.

Build

Step of taking all idea and transfer it to the paper or digital.

Test

Test as soon as possible. Step of testing everything that was built.

Validate

Step of analyze all insights got from test and order it by need/criticality.

Follow

Follow the development. Critical step to ensure the fidelity between design and development.
