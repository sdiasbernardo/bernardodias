Simple2u

A new 100% digital on demand insurance company.

Brief

Simple2u comes to change the way we buy and use insurance products. The user can choose when he wants to activate or deactivate it based on selected day, hour or location.

Design process

Methodologies used and its deliverables.

User Centered Design Canvas: first team meeting rounds of business analysis, ideas alignments and user experience organization.

CSD Matrix: we got all certainties, suppositions, and doubts about the business to help us start development a more assertive product.

Mapping of user journeys and their touchpoints with the product/service.

Benchmark of insurance market and our competitors.

Shorthand userflow: a method of designing better and easier to understand flows.

First usability testing report from the purchase flow tested on wireframe stage.

The solution

Complete build solution of web, hybrid app, landing page, blog and e-mails.
