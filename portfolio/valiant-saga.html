Valiant Saga

A game with a classic 2d feeling that mixes pixel art with stunning modern effects.

Brief

Valiant Saga is a game project that I'm developing with a friend called Fernando. We are using the famous Unreal Engine to develop it.

Valiant Saga has built a solid audience and is being developed to run on PC, Mac, Linux, PS4/PS5, Xbox One/Xbox Series and Nintendo Switch.

The game was quickly approved on Greenlight - Steam publishing system where the users votes which games will be sold on the platform.

The Greenlight campaign generated more than 8300 unique visitors, 680 comments and 484 additions to the wish list.

Game features

Immersive adventure inspired by retro platform games.

Visceral combat

Unleash the power of the inherited axe from your master and defeat without mercy all enemies that dare to stay in your way.

Epic battles

To stay alive you will need to overcome the most terrible creatures and they will be increasingly deadly and dangerous.

RPG features

You will be able to help people from villages in side quests, solve challenging puzzles and collect different types of items that will support you along your journey.

The destiny of the braves

You will need to pass over several traps through dungeons and abandoned temples. The fear will be your only company.

Retro style

Valiant Saga follows the classic platform games but with pixelated art mixed with modern lighting and shading effects.

Freedom and exploration

The game has a level progression system accessed through a map with all kingdoms that you can go back to search for new secrets or hidden places.
