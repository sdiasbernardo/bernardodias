Life Design

A unique design system built with solid standards.

The problem

The four pain points and problems that Life Design solved.

1

Lack of a solid digital identity and too basic / generic style guide.

2

High effort of design team prototyping new product interface for each new project.

3

High effort of development team coding new product interface for each new project.

4

Too much time spent with adjustments of designed interface > developed result.

High divergences in digital products. Components were not shared between projects.

The Three Principles

The principles that makes Life Design an alive design system.

Life Design evolves

Elements can go through the four lifecycle stages:

Borning

The simple stage where the element is generated on the screen (comes to life).

Growth

The ability of an element to growth/evolve.

Reproduction

Elements can generate another.

Death

The element no longer exist on the screen.

Complete build solution of web, hybrid app, landing page, blog and e-mails.
