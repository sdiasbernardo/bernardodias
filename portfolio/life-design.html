Life Design

A unique design system built with solid standards.

The problem

The four pain points and problems that Life Design solved.

1

Lack of a solid digital identity and too basic / generic style guide.

2

High effort of design team prototyping new product interface for each new project.

3

High effort of development team coding new product interface for each new project.

4

Too much time spent with adjustments of designed interface > developed result.

High divergences in digital products. Components were not shared between projects.

The Three Principles

The principles that makes Life Design an alive design system.

Life Design evolves

Elements can go through the four lifecycle stages:

The simple stage where the element is generated on the screen (comes to life).

The ability of an element to growth/evolve.

Elements can generate another.

The element no longer exist on the screen.

Life Design moves

Life-enriching state, movement brings unique experiences and provides better feedback.

Life Design responds

Elements have the ability to respond to actions (feedback) through movements, transitions, growth or color change.

Life Design Base

Life Design has two pillars that guides the design foundation:

Golden Ratio

The Golden Ratio it's responsible to guide and build the components, such as font sizing, line heighting, content and sections spacings and icons construction grid.

Complexion Reduction

Complexion Reduction trend brings to a Life Design the black and white predominance with colors being applied in an objective way.

Golden Ratio font fundamentals it's a way to get better readable and harmonic texts.

Golden Ratio applied in sections and components spacing.

Icons are created using the golden ratio rectangle and its proportions.

Colors are applied with three objectives: guide the user to an action, provide feedback and brand reinforcement.

The solution

A complete design system that powers all the products, with Figma and Adobe XD design files, Angular and React components and Design System website.
